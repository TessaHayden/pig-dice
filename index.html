Pig Die

A 2-player game, first player to a 100 points, wins!

Rules of the game:
    If the first player rolls a 1:
  • The first player does not score and it is the next players turn.
    • If the first player rolls 2-6, the face value of the die will be added to the player's score.
  • The first player may roll again and accumulate more points
  • Or the first player can hold, keep the points they've won and it is the second player's turn.

    • NOTE: If a player rolls a 1, the player's points are cleared and the player starts back at 0.
